Preventing Athletic Injuries

Knee

Myofascial Release

Quads (Foam Roll)

3 x 30 sec each. Roll back and forth over quads, flex and extend for added pressure.

Hamstrings (Foam Roll)

3 x 30 sec each.

Adductors (Foam Roll)

3 x 30 sec each.

IT Band (Foam Roll)

3 x 30 sec each.

Stretching

Side Lunge (Adductors)

3 x 30 sec each.

Quad Stretch

3 x 30 sec each. Grab foot or ankle and pull backwards. Keep knee close to standing leg.

Hamstring Stretch

3 x 30 sec each.

Strengthening

Leg Curls

3 x 10 each. Lie prone to flex and extend knee.

Heel Slides

3 x 10 each. Use socks or towel under foot. Drive heel into ground while bringing it towards body and extend.

Quad Sets

3 x 10 each four ways. Squeeze quad muscles and slowly lower down, with dorsiflexed ankle.

Step Ups

3 x 10 each.
